## üí∞ Installing a wallet

##### ‚ùìWhat is a web3 wallet?

A web3 wallet is a digital wallet that allows users to securely store, manage and interact with their cryptocurrencies and other digital assets (e.g NFTs) on the decentralized web (Web3).

#####  üíª Installing a web3 wallet browser extension

Here are the general steps to install a web3 wallet browser extension:

1. Open your preferred web browser (Chrome, Firefox, etc.).
2. Go to the browser extension store (e.g. Chrome Web Store for Google Chrome).
3. Search for the web3 wallet extension you want to install (e.g. [MetaMask](https://metamask.io/), [Coinbase Wallet](https://www.coinbase.com/wallet/articles/getting-started-extension) etc.).
4. Click on the "Add to Chrome/Firefox" button or similar.
5. A pop-up window may appear, click on "Add Extension" or similar.
6. Wait for the extension to finish installing.
7. Once the extension is installed, a new icon will appear in your browser's toolbar.
8. Click on the extension icon to set up your wallet by following the on-screen instructions.

## üîó Connecting your wallet

Here are the simple steps to connect a wallet to our dApp using the "Connect Wallet" button:

1. Open the Web3 dApp in your web browser.
2. Look for the "Connect Wallet" button in top right corner of any Unick dApp 
3. Click on the "Connect Wallet" button.
4. A pop-up window will appear, showing a list of supported wallets.
5. Select the wallet you want to connect (e.g. MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet).
6. If you haven't installed the wallet extension yet, follow the instructions above to install it.
7. Once the wallet is installed and connected, you may need to unlock it by entering your password or passphrase.
{''}
Unick dApp should now be connected to your wallet, and you can interact with it using your wallet's features.
